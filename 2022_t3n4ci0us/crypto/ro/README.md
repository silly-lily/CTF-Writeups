#### Flag
> t3n4ci0us{540}

First we let `W E = 360 = S N S = 360`, then we solve for `S` to get `S = (W E)-N/2 = 180-N/2`. Next we have to guess `N`. We guess `N = 1` and let `N E W S = W E S N S - S = 360+360-180/N = 360+360-180 = 540` which is our flag.
