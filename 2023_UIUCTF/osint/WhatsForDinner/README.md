# Whats For Dinner
Whats For Dinner is an osint challenge where we need to find the online profile of Jonah Explorer who was spotted eating at a joyful Italian restaurant in West Loop, Chicago. We also know that Jonah is a food and travel blogger who enjoys reviewing restaurants and document his travel.

## Initial Google Search
We decide to first identify the resturant that Jonah ate at. We first search for an Italian restaurant in West Loop, Chicago: `italian west loop chicago`. However, there appears to be many Italian restaurants there.

![Initial Search](search.png)

## Refined Google Search
We know we need to sort through these Italian restaurants and notice that the challenge described the restaurant as `joyful`, so we try the search again with this information: `joyful`.

![Gioia Restaurant](gioia.png)

## Yelp Search
After finding the Gioia Restaurant, we know that Jonah may have eaten there and left a review. We also know that a common website to leave food reviews on is Yelp. So we find the Gioia Restaurant on Yelp which is listed as Gioia Ristorante Pastificio (a slightly different name). We then check the reviews and we find a review by Jonah E.

![Jonah Explorer's Yelp Review](yelp.png)
